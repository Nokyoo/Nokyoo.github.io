Asnières, the Heart of Louis Vuitton

Explore the historical Louis Vuitton Maison in Asnières-sur-Seine.

Louis Vuitton's Historic Workshop

The Louis Vuitton Maison in Asnières, an iconic location in the Parisian region, combines the founder’s Art Nouveau home with traditional trunk-making workshops and a renovated gallery space.

The Foundation of a Legacy

In 1859, just five years after founding his eponymous brand, Louis Vuitton moved his workshop to Asnières-sur-Seine. This location provided convenient access to the Seine, which enabled easy transportation of wood, a key material for trunk-making. Today, the site continues to craft special orders for clients worldwide and serves as a hub for prototyping fashion pieces.

Exhibition: La Malle Courrier

The gallery at Asnières showcases the famous "Malle Courrier" trunk in a modern exhibition, celebrating its innovative and lightweight design from 1858. This timeless creation embodies the Maison’s spirit of travel and luxury.

Exhibition Dates: May 23, 2023 - February 17, 2024

Inside LVMH Program

LVMH has launched "Inside LVMH," a unique initiative for students from prestigious European schools and universities, as well as for interns and apprentices. This digital platform allows young talents to engage with strategic issues and imagine the future of luxury experiences. The top participants have opportunities to join the 6,500 interns and 1,000 graduates recruited annually by the group.
